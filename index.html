Designer Advocate - Speaker - Mentor

Hey there! I'm Matt Rae, a designer & community ambassador based out of Waterloo, ON, Canada. I'm a designer advocate at Adobe working on Adobe XD. I work closely with the design community, designers and design teams to help them harness the power of XD in their workflows. I'm also available for Enterprise design team training.

My career in ux/product design has spanned a breadth of industries. I co-founded a travel marketplace for local experiences after graduating school, and honed my craft. Since then I've worked across EdTech at Chalk.com, Autonomous vehicles with OTTO Motors., and most recently AdTech with Zenreach.
