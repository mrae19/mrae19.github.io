Matt Rae

Product Designer. Community Ambassador. Adobe XDI.

Hey there! I'm Matt Rae, a designer & community ambassador based out of Waterloo, ON, Canada. I'm currently part of the Design team at Zenreach, focused on bridging the marketing and advertising gap for brick and mortart retailers in North America. I'm also a member of the Adobe XDI (XD Instructors) team here in North America, teachinig workshops for enterprise design teams.

Previously I worked with the talented team at OTTO Motors, building the leading autonomous industrial vehicles in market today. As a senior member of the design team we worked on problems spanning software UX to human-robot interaction.

Prior to that I led product development at Chalk, focused on developing the next generation of educational software tools. Using data and a strong focus on teacher and student experience our vision was to give teacher's the tools and information that needed to do what they do best; teach. Our small team developed applications that revolutionized curriculum distribution, assessment, and lesson planning.

I'm passionate about growing the design community, both locally in Canada, and more broadly. The DesignChats community started in 2015 over breakfast with fellow designers and has been evolving into an incredible network of designers.

Interested in learning more? I'm always happy to make myself available for a chat about design. Whether you're just getting started and want some tips, or are looking for guidance on how to develop or grow a design team at your organization, send me a note.
